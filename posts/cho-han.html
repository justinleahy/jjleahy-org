Chō-Han (丁半)

A traditional Japenese gambling game written in C++ using the olcPixelGameEngine

Posted by Justin Leahy on June 8, 2021

Chō-Han is a Japenese gambling game played by rolling two dice while betting odd or even.

In this game, you change your bet using the UP and DOWN arrow keys, and change your option with LEFT and RIGHT. Press ENTER to confirm your bet and the results of the dice are revealed.

The goal is just to accumlate as much money as possible while not reaching zero.

I honestly only wrote this because it's a good coding practice and was inspired by javidx9's recent video on Five Dice.
